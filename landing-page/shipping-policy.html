Shipping Policy

Last updated: December 21, 2024

1. Digital Delivery

Quixhr is a SaaS (Software as a Service) platform. We do not sell physical products that require shipping. Upon successful payment and registration, you will receive immediate access to your account and the services purchased.

2. Account Activation

Account activation details will be sent to the email address provided during registration. If you do not receive an email within 15 minutes of purchase, please check your spam folder or contact our support team.

3. International Access

Our services are available globally. As a digital product, there are no shipping restrictions or customs fees.
