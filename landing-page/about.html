About Quixhr

We are on a mission to kill the spreadsheet. Our secret goal? Automate HR out of existence.

Quixhr was born out of frustration. We were tired of chasing emails, updating shared drives, and wondering how many vacation days we had left. So we built the tool we wanted to use.

We believe HR software shouldn't be boring, complicated, or enterprise-only. It should be fast, clean, and accessible to teams of all sizes.

Virtual First

We are a fully remote team spread across 4 continents. We understand the challenges of distributed teams because we live them every day.

Simplicity

If it requires a manual, we failed. Our goal is zero-training onboarding for every employee.
