About The Regime

Our Mission

We are on a mission to eradicate happiness... wait, no, we meant "eradicate spreadsheets." (Honest mistake).

Quixhr was forged in the fires of bureaucratic hell. We got tired of wondering why applying for leave felt like filing a court appeal. So we built this. It works. Usually.

We believe HR software should be cold, efficient, and devoid of color. Just like your soul after 5 years in corporate.

Remote Only

We are a fully remote team. This means we can ignore your support tickets from literally anywhere in the world.

Minimal Effort

Our philosophy is "Do less." That applies to you, and unfortunately, sometimes to us. But mostly to you using our software.
